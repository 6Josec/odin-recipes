Flaming Cheese

Get your cheese on!

Ingredients

  • 500g of cheese (mix of montery and chedar)
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes (300ml)
  • 1/2 pound of ground chorizo

Intructions

  1. shred the cheese
  2. cook the chorizo
  3. add the tomatoes
  4. add the cheese
  5. simmer till the liquid has evaporated
  6. om nom nom
