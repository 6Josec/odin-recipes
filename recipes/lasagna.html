Sweet Delicious Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 box of long flat noodle things
  • 1 pot of homeade pasta sauce (stolen from your neighbour)
  • 1 container of cottage cheese
  • 2 bricks of cheese (one of each cheddar and mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Steal the pot of homeade pasta sauce
  2. Shred the cheese
  3. Layer the noodles, sauce, and cottage cheese (making about 3 layers)
  4. Add the shredded cheese to the top
  5. Bake at 350 degrees farenheit for 2 hours
