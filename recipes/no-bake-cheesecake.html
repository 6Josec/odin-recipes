Quick and Scrumptious Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 2 packages cream cheese
  • 1 carton whipping cream
  • 2 table spoons sugar
  • 5 table spoons butter
  • 1 box crushed graham crackers
  • 2 cans cherry pie filling

Instructions

  1. Add the craham cracker to a large baking tray
  2. Melt the butter and mix in into the graham crackers
  3. Bake graham crackers at 350 for 20 minutes
  4. Whisk the cream and sugar together until texture thickens
  5. Add the cream cheese to your mixture and whisk until smooth
  6. Let graham crackers cool and add cream cheese filling
  7. Add cherry pie filling to top
  8. Consume
